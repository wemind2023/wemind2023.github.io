What is Wemind?

Wemind connects donors and beneficiaries within a virtual gift economy that allows donors to monitor their donations impact and receive tokens equivalent to the value they have created. Reciprocity incites beneficiaries to exchange these tokens for goods and services, making the tokens valuable. Moreover, tokens received by donors can be automatically returned to to the system and create more impact than traditional donations.

How does Wemind keep track of my donations?

Wemind connects +100 NGOs, thousands of young people in need, +200 startups and +300 SMEs with donators. All donations we receive are tracked by gathering feedback from our community and directly brought to your dashboard.
