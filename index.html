What is Wemind?

Wemind connects donators and beneficiaries within a virtual gift economy that allows donators to monitor their impact and receive tokens equivalent to the value they have created. Reciprocity incites beneficiaries to accept these tokens and exchange them for goods and services, making them valuable. Moreover, tokens received by donators can be automatically returned to to the ecosystem and create more impact than traditional donations.

How does Wemind keep track of my donations?

Wemind connects you with +100 NGOs, thousands of young people in need, +200 startups and +300 SMEs with donators. All donations we receive are tracked by gathering feedback from our community and directly brought to your dashboard.

How do beneficiaries exchange Wemind tokens?

Wemind learns your business. By analyzing your sales data, Hydra optimizes your sales process and show you where you should be spending your resources.

Wemind's tokenization process not only incentivizes participation but also strength-ens the emotional loyalty bond between donors and beneficiaries, fostering a sense of shared value creation and community-building in line with the principles of the Gift Economy.
